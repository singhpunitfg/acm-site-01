About 

DIT University

DIT University is a renowned university in a celebrated city where knowledge meets achievement, history meets the future and ambition meets inspiration. It is designed to provide holistic education, enabling students to stand on their own feet. The focus is always on promotion of self-learning and honing talents to ensure the all-round development amongst the students. Students are encouraged to actively organize and participate in various club activities and assist the Institute as responsible members of various academic committees to help impart leadership skills.

Association for Computing Machinery

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is an international learned society for computing. As the world’s largest computing society, ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence. Founded at the dawn of the computer age, ACM’s reach extends to every part of the globe, with more than half of its more than 100,000 members residing outside the U.S. Its growing membership has led to Councils in Europe, India, and China, fostering networking opportunities that strengthen ties within and across countries and technical communities. ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life‐long learning, career development, and professional networking.

DITU ACM Student Chapter

Founded in 2018, DITU ACM Student Chapter is a technical society of DITU, Dehradun. With the assistance of one of the most outstanding organizations in the computing world, ACM, we strive towards a single goal i.e. Advance computing as a science and profession. With some of the most eminent minds of DITU working intently as the backbone of the society, we are sparing no efforts. Organizing classes, coding challenges, quizzes, hackathons and workshops for our students, the activities of the chapter are widely acknowledged in the institute and keenly looked for. DITU ACM Student Chapter comprises of several teams which help the students grow and learn of their interests and where they can excel such as programming, IOT, Data Science, Cyber Security, Web and Design, and App development, etc.

DITU ACM-W Student Chapter

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women. DITU ACM-W provides a platform for the sharing of information, resources, ideas and experiences, supporting the students in their professional career growth and help them to face the challenges in their work environment. Its main objective is to encourage women to participate in computer-related studies and research.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the benefit of joining the DITU ACM student chapter?
Joining the DITU ACM student chapter has many benefits, including access to exclusive events, networking opportunities, and resources that can help you enhance your skills and advance your career. You'll also get to meet other students who share your interests and passion for computer science and technology.
How many teams are there in the DITU ACM student chapter?
We have several teams in the DITU ACM student chapter, each focused on a different area of computer science and technology. Some of our teams include the programming team, the cybersecurity team, and the design team, among others.
How can I stay up to date on the DITU ACM student chapter activities and events?
To stay up to date on DITU ACM student chapter activities and events, you can follow us on social media or sign up for our email newsletter.
What is the purpose of the DITU ACM student chapter?
The purpose of the DITU ACM student chapter is to provide students with opportunities to explore and learn more about computer science and technology. We aim to foster a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about technology and to help our members enhance their skills and advance their careers.
Can I attend DITU ACM student chapter events if I am not a member?
While we encourage students to become members of the DITU ACM student chapter to take full advantage of our resources and opportunities, many of our events are open to non-members as well. Check out our website and social media pages for details about specific events.
My question isn't listed here!
If you have questions that aren't listed here, please feel free to reach out to us directly! You can message us on our social media pages and we'll be happy to assist you.
